Parents Ask Us 🤔

Common questions answered

🧒 What is the minimum age?

We accept children starting from 2 years old for our Pre-Nursery program.

⏰ What are the timings?

Our fun-filled day starts at 10:00 AM and wraps up at 1:30 PM (Monday to Friday).

🍎 Is food provided?

We ask parents to pack healthy snacks. We provide filtered water and occasional treats!

🚌 Do you provide transport?

Currently, we don't provide transportation. Parents drop and pick up their children.

👥 Teacher-student ratio?

We maintain a ratio of 1:10 to ensure personalized attention for every child.

📚 What curriculum do you follow?

We follow a play-based, child-centered curriculum that prepares kids for formal schooling.
